Zuma Experience

A-rods Zuma experience:

On arrival your greeted by the front of house doormen, who welcome you and open the magnificant large glass doors. upon entering you blown away with the vibrant atmosphere and high energy.

The decor is slick, sharp and clean cut. We are acknowledge by the zuma front of house who are very busy but still make sure you are noticed.

After a short wait we head the bar which is busy with people drinking their cockails, wine and champagnee. The service is plesant friendly and fast. The creativity and speed that the drinks are made is breathe taking its like watching art (Love it)

After ordering our drinks we make our order which includes one of the days specials so we are vey excited to see and taste
